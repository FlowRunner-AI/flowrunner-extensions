# Mailbox FlowRunner Extension

Comprehensive email management solution for reading, sending, and managing emails through SMTP and IMAP protocols.

## Ideal Use Cases

- Automated email monitoring and processing workflows
- Customer support ticket management and auto-responses
- Marketing email campaign management and tracking
- Email-based data collection and lead generation
- Notification systems and alert management
- Email archiving and backup automation
- Multi-account email synchronization
- Automated email classification and filtering

## List of Actions

- Mark Email As Unread
- Read Inbox
- Send Email
