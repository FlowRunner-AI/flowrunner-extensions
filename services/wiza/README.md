# Wiza FlowRunner Extension

Advanced lead generation and contact enrichment platform for building targeted prospect lists and automating outreach preparation workflows.

## Ideal Use Cases

- Automated LinkedIn profile enrichment and contact discovery
- Targeted prospect list building and lead generation
- Contact data validation and email verification
- Sales pipeline automation and lead qualification
- CRM integration and contact database management
- Outbound marketing campaign preparation
- Market research and competitor analysis
- API credit monitoring and usage optimization
