# Facebook Lead Ads FlowRunner Extension

Complete Facebook advertising platform integration for creating, managing, and optimizing lead generation campaigns. Build comprehensive ad workflows from campaign creation to lead form management.

## Ideal Use Cases

- Creating and managing comprehensive Facebook ad campaigns for lead generation
- Building automated lead capture workflows with custom forms and follow-up sequences
- Managing ad account performance tracking and campaign optimization
- Developing targeted advertising campaigns with detailed audience segmentation
- Creating dynamic ad creatives with lead generation forms integration
- Automating campaign budget management and performance monitoring
- Building multi-stage advertising funnels with ad sets and creative variations
- Integrating Facebook lead ads with CRM systems and marketing automation
