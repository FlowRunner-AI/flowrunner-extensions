# HubSpot FlowRunner Extension

Complete HubSpot CRM integration for managing contacts, companies, deals, and sales pipelines. Build automated workflows for lead management, customer relationships, and business development processes.

## Ideal Use Cases

- Automating lead generation and contact management workflows
- Managing sales pipelines with automated deal tracking and updates
- Building customer relationship management systems with contact data synchronization
- Creating automated company profile management and association workflows
- Integrating HubSpot data with marketing automation and lead nurturing campaigns
- Building sales reporting and analytics systems with real-time data access
- Managing customer support workflows with contact and company data integration
- Automating data entry and CRM maintenance tasks with intelligent data processing
