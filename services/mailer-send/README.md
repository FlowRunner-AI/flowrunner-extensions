# MailerSend FlowRunner Extension

Professional email delivery platform with comprehensive tracking, template support, and real-time webhook notifications for email automation workflows.

## Ideal Use Cases

- Transactional email delivery with advanced tracking capabilities
- Marketing email campaigns with template personalization
- E-commerce order confirmations and shipping notifications
- User onboarding and engagement email sequences
- Real-time email event monitoring and analytics
- Automated email responses based on recipient behavior
- Multi-recipient communication with CC and BCC support
- Email deliverability optimization and spam prevention

## List of Actions

- Send Email
- Send Email with CC/BCC
- Send Email with Template

## List of Triggers

- On Email Delivered
- On Email Not Delivered
- On Email Opened
- On Email Opened First
- On Email Sent
- On Email Soft Bounced
- On Link Clicked
- On Link Clicked First
- On Recipient Unsubscribed
- On Spam Complaint
