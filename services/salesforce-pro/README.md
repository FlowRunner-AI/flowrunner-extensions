# Salesforce PRO FlowRunner Extension

Comprehensive Salesforce CRM integration with advanced features for complex sales operations, lead conversion, and enterprise-level automation workflows.

## Ideal Use Cases

- Advanced lead management and conversion workflows
- Complex sales opportunity tracking and reporting
- Multi-record relationship management and line items
- Enterprise email automation and communication
- Advanced file and attachment management
- Custom SOQL query execution and data analysis
- Parent-child record relationship automation
- Comprehensive contact and lead lifecycle management
