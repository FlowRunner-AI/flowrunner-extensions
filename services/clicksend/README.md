# ClickSend FlowRunner Extension

Comprehensive communication platform integration enabling SMS, MMS, voice messages, fax, and postal mail delivery. Manage contact lists and launch multi-channel campaigns with ease.

## Ideal Use Cases

- Sending transactional SMS notifications for order updates and alerts
- Managing customer contact lists with automated campaigns
- Delivering voice messages for appointment reminders and confirmations
- Sending physical postcards and letters for marketing campaigns
- Creating multi-channel communication workflows combining SMS, MMS, and voice
- Building customer support notification systems with multiple delivery methods
- Automating bulk messaging campaigns to targeted contact segments
- Integrating fax delivery for document-heavy business processes
